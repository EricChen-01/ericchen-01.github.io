About

Coded using Python and Discord.py, KAYLE BOT is a party bot for discord.

Currently, KAYLE has a handful of commands that allows users to express

themselves better through images and emojis. KAYLE also has her very own

AI chat system that allows users to interact with her and even pull things

from the internet.
